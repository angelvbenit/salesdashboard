# Sales & Profitability Performance Dashboard
# Project Overview
This interactive Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profitability trends, and logistics efficiency. By transforming raw sales data into actionable insights, this tool allows stakeholders to monitor key business drivers, identify underperforming products, and evaluate regional performance.

![Sales Dashboard Preview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/angelvbenit/salesdashboard/main/salesdashboard.png)

# Key Features & Insights
Executive Summary (KPIs): Real-time tracking of Total Sales ($1.92M), Total Profit ($224K), and an overall Profit Margin of 11.64%.

Sales & Profitability Trend: A dual-axis combination chart (Bar & Line) visualizing the monthly relationship between revenue volume and margin percentage.

Logistics Analysis: Insights into shipping efficiency with an average 2-day ship time and distribution of shipment modes (Regular Air, Express Air, and Delivery Truck).

Geographic & Product Performance: * Identification of Top 5 Profitable States and Top 10 Cities.

Critical analysis of the "Top 3 Least Profitable Products" to pinpoint inventory or pricing issues.

Manager Leaderboard: A comparative view of performance across regional managers, highlighting Sam’s profitability challenges vs. Erin’s top performance.

# Technical Skills Applied
Data Modeling: Utilizing Power Pivot to establish relationships between tables.

Advanced Calculations: Creating calculated fields and measures for Profit Margin % and Return Rate %.

Dynamic Visualizations: Customizing charts (Donut, Dual-Axis, Horizontal Bar) for maximum readability.

Interactivity: Integrated Slicers for Order Date, Customer Segment, Order Priority, and Product Category to allow for deep-dive exploratory analysis.

# Tools Used
Microsoft Excel: Power Pivot, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Advanced Slicers.
